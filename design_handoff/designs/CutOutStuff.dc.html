FAQ

Questions, answered.

What size can my cutout be?+

Anywhere from a 24-inch tabletop version up to a full life-size standee over six feet tall. We scale to the real subject and confirm dimensions on your proof.

Can I order just one cutout?+

Absolutely. There's no minimum, a single custom cutout is our most common order.

Can I ship cutouts to multiple people?+

Yes. For campaigns we support direct fulfillment, each order ships straight to its own recipient with tracking.

What image quality do I need?+

A sharp, well-lit, high-resolution photo is ideal. We review every image before production and will tell you if anything needs improving.

Can you remove a background?+

Yes, background removal and cleanup are part of standard file preparation, included at no extra charge.

Do you offer discreet packaging?+

Yes. Every order can ship in plain, unbranded protective packaging on request.

Can creators sell through CutOutStuff?+

Yes. Approved creators can launch a private campaign page, we handle production, fulfillment, and shipping. See the Creator Program.

What if my cutout arrives damaged?+

Send us a photo and we'll produce a replacement. See our Return & Replacement Policy.

Do I need to own the rights to the image?+

Yes. You must own or control the rights to any image you submit. See our Content & Image Rights Policy.
