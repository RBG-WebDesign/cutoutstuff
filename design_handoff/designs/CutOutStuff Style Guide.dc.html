05 · Spacing & layout

Container
Max-width 1200px (marketing), 1160px (dashboard). Gutter clamp(20px, 5vw, 40px).
Section rhythm
Vertical padding clamp(56px, 8vw, 110px). Generous whitespace over dense packing.
Grid seams
Card grids use 1px gaps over a hairline rgba(23,25,28,0.09) to form crisp seams.

06 · Voice

Do
“Bring them into the room.”
“Built to be displayed, not thrown away.”
Lead with the occasion and the emotion (the person, pet, or moment), not the fabrication. Calm, premium, celebratory.
Avoid
“Monetize your super-fans.”
Hype, pressure, or anything that makes a buyer feel judged.
Exclamation-heavy marketing speak and emoji.
